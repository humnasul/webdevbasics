Kayaking
Kayaks were originally used by artic inhabitants. Our events use single-seater kayaks, with a maximum weight of 100 lbs. The participants must provide the proper documentation to the officials, one week before the start of the event.

Bicycles
Any type of 2-wheel racing-approved bicycle can be used for the event. A more formal description of racing bicycles is available upon request. The participants must submit the proper safety documentation of their cycling equipment one month before the start of the event.

Canoes
Canoes are used in the final leg of the race. Canoes must adhere to the description to be provided to each participant, two weeks before the start of the race.  The weight of the canoe cannot exceed 150 lbs., and safety documentation is supposed to be submitted one week before the start of the event.
