New York City

The Big Apple



New York City is one of the most famous cities in the world. It is primarily known for its liveliness and bustling crowds. The city is full of activities and opportunities.
Below are some important historical events for the city:

  1. Brooklyn Bridge Construction
  2. The Statue of Liberty Established
  3. NYC became United Nation's headquarters

The city is filled with essential buildings and worldwide businesses. It is a hub for communication and development. It is often referred to as the "capitol of the world" for its international connections and relevance. New York City is also the most linguistically diverse city in the world and has the largest foreign-born population of any city in the world as of 2016.
The following provides you with more information:

  • World Trade Center
  • Empire State Building
  • Central Park

New York City is often collectively referred to as the Five Boroughs. These Five Boroughs are Brooklyn, Queens, Manhattan, the Bronx, and Staten Island. Each of them are densely populated and have their own historical culture and origin. Recreational activities and parks are an essential part of the communities.
